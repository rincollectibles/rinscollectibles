Mobile Collectible Sales & Appraisals

Why pack up your fragile or bulky collections? We offer a convenient mobile service where we travel to your location to browse, buy, or sell collectibles.

  • Step 1: Get in Touch. Contact us via phone or email with a brief description of the collection you want to sell or the items you are looking to buy.
  • Step 2: Schedule a Time. We will find a date and time that works best for your schedule.
  • Step 3: The Mobile Visit. We arrive at your location, provide fair market appraisals, and facilitate hassle-free transactions on the spot.
